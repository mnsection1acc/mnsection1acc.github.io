Information needed

  • Fill out the section meet team roster on athletic.net for the Section 1A Championship Meet by 8:00 PM on Tuesday, October 21, 2025. All participants in the section meet must be one of the nine names listed entered on the athletic.net section meet team roster.
  • There is a fee of $20.00 per runner participating in the section meet (maximum of 7 runners per boys/girls team). Please have your school send a check to the Region 1A Secretary, Brad Johnson (3570 Stone Point Drive NE, Rochester, MN 55906). Checks should be made out to MSHSL Region 1A. If at all possible, please have your activities director send the check before the section meet.
  • Fill out the section meet uniform verification Google Form (see link above).
  • Obtain a doctor's permission form for any runner that may use an inhaler during the race. An approval form from the MSHSL is not required.
  • Obtain an approval from the MSHSL for any athlete using an insulin pump or heart monitor during the race. This form must be submitted to the meet manager before the race.

Before the Race

  • Remind runners to bring any liquids or food they will use before or after the race.
  • Remind runners to bring $20 if they would like a T-shirt.
  • Remind runners they cannot go on the course until 1:30 PM on the day of the meet.

Arrival at the Race (NOTE: Coaches' Meeting - 2:15 PM)

  1. Pick up your school's packet at the golf course clubhouse porch.
  2. The envelopes contain the runners' numbers and pins. Pass out the numbers to the runners. The packet will specify which number has been assigned to each runner. In addition, each number has a label on the pulltag with the runner's name. The numbers are to be placed on the front of the runner's shirt using 4 pins. Pins for are included in the packet. Additional pins are available at the registration table.
  3. The bibs will have disposable tags attached that are used for timing. The bibs do not have to be returned.
  4. Coaches' meeting will be at 2:15 PM outside the golf course clubhouse (or inside if inclement weather).
  5. Golf carts will not be available for the coaches.

Start of the Race

  1. Runners should be at the starting area 15 minutes before the start. Officials will check that race numbers are properly attached and will look for any obvious uniform rule violations.
  2. Lane assignments are in the packets. Note that the lane assignments for the girls and boys races are different.
  3. Runners will be allowed to warm up in front of their lane until one minute before the start. Make sure to review with your runners the starting procedures described in the document link above. The announcements won't necessarily follow the printed procedures, but all of the procedures from two minutes before the start will be followed.

Awards Ceremony

  • The first and second place teams will receive 10 medals for the 9 team members and the coach.
  • The top ten individuals will receive medals. The first six individual finishers who are not members of the first and second place qualifying teams advance to the state meet. Any state qualifying individual who does not place in the top ten will also receive a state qualifying medal.
  • Coaches of teams and individuals qualifying for state need to obtain the state tournament information from the coaches' dashboard when signed into the MSHSL web site www.mshsl.org.
